Put it all together

We can pipe commands together. Think of a pipe (i.e. %>%) as a conjunction. Any time you see a pipe, think “and then.”

cars %>% 
  filter(cyl >= 4, cyl <= 6) %>% 
  select(makeModel, mpg:wt) %>%
  mutate(dispWt = disp / wt) %>% 
  arrange(desc(mpg), wt)
